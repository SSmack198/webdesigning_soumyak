About FashionEra


FASHIONERA is an ecommerce portal with a difference. It is a men-only, managed marketplace with a zero inventory model, showcasing over 750 big & small brands and 25,000+ user generated fashion looks. What makes FASHIONERA stand apart is its creative ideas to provide the best experience for customers. FASHIONERA is the first shopping website to provide an exclusive social platform for men. The brand is making a conscious effort to bring the power of fashion to shoppers with an array of the latest and trendiest products available in the country. We approach in the hybrid logistic model.
